1. Alimentatie sanatoasa si echilibrata!

A healthy diet means avoiding fast foods as much as possible, or cranberries cooked in too much oil! (* Quite painful *)

In a healthy diet it is recommended to have 3 meals a day (breakfast; lunch; dinner), and between them if we feel the need for something sweet, a fruit is perfect (avoid too much sugar)!

The healthiest foods are generally those that come directly from nature, devoid of certain processes.

Try to eat before dinner at 21:00, for a much better digestion!

APA has a very important role in nutrition, being recommended an amount of 1.5 - 2 l / day.

A healthy diet is based on:

  • Fruits, vegetables
  • Lean meat: fish, chicken, pork, red meat
  • Low fat dairy products
  • Whole grain bakery
  • Oils: olive and coconut

``Very important: avoid hunger! ``

2. The sport!

This is the second most important criterion after eating!

If you play a performance sport, or spend at least an hour every day for sports, this is great!

But for those who are not very athletic, you can start the first days with only 5 minutes of sports, and increase the duration along the way!

Even if you don't have a gym membership, there are plenty of other options:

  • Running
  • Cardio exercises at home (there are a lot of varieties on the internet)
  • Weightless muscle mass exercises (there are a lot of varieties on the internet)
  • Go on foot or by bike to the service, instead of the car!
  • Choose the stairs, not the elevator!
  • Walking with bigger steps! (Running step)

However, if you want you can try at least a month at the gym, this can bring you more motivation when you see more people with the same goal in the same place!

In fact, practicing a favorite sport can bring you more results, depending on your involvement!

``Very important: before any workout, warm-up is essential !``
